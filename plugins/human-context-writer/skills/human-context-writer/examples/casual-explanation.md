# Casual Explanation Example

## User request

Explain why AI writing sometimes feels robotic.

## Weak AI-style answer

AI writing can feel robotic because it often uses structured language, formal tone, and generalized explanations. This can make the output less relatable to the reader.

## Human-style answer

AI writing often feels robotic because it is too clean. The sentences are neat, the structure is predictable, and the tone is usually too neutral. A real person normally has a point of view. They might be unsure, annoyed, excited, or direct. That small messiness is what makes writing feel alive.

## Notes

A good casual explanation should:

- start directly
- avoid over-explaining
- use simple words
- sound like someone is actually talking
