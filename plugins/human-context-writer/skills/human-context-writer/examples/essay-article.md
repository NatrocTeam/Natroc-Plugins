# Essay and Article Writing Example

## User request

Write a short article intro about why most developer tools have bad documentation.

## Weak AI-style intro

In the rapidly evolving world of software development, documentation plays a crucial role in ensuring that developers can effectively utilize the tools at their disposal. However, many developer tools continue to struggle with providing adequate and comprehensive documentation for their users.

## Human-style intro

Most developer tools have bad documentation because the people who built the tool already know how to use it. They document what the tool does, not what a new user needs to understand before they can use it.

The result is documentation that answers the wrong questions. It tells you how to configure a flag that you will not need until week three, and skips over the part that everyone gets stuck on in week one.

## Notes

A good article intro should:

- open with a real, specific observation — not a broad claim about an "evolving world"
- have a clear point of view
- be specific enough to feel grounded
- set up the article without over-explaining what is about to be said
- avoid symmetrical paragraphs and generic academic framing
